What We Do At iMANISchools.

iMANI schools is a problem solving platform, developed and designed to aid most issues faced in Universities & Schools by its students and staff across africa. Below are a few out of many services we offer:

Virtual Learning Resources

iMANI could offer a comprehensive virtual learning environment equipped with e-books, lecture recordings, and interactive study materials to support students' academic endeavors.

Resource Tracking System

iMANI could implement a resource tracking system that enables students and staff to easily locate and reserve library books, equipment, or facilities, optimizing resource utilization.

Information Portal

iMANI could serve as a centralized information portal, providing access to important announcements, event schedules, academic calendars, and university policies in a user-friendly format.

Technology Integration Solutions

iMANI could offer technology integration solutions tailored to the university's needs, including digital classrooms, online assessments, and administrative automation tools to enhance productivity and innovation.
